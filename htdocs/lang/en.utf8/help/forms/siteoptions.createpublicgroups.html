Publicly viewable team work spaces

If you wish to allow all users to create work spaces that may be viewed by people who are not members of your site, set this option to 'Everyone'.

In a public work space, anyone who finds your site (including search engines) will be able to see the work space's "About" page, and all discussions in the forums. However, people will still have to become a member of the site before they can perform other actions such as posting to the forums or viewing user profiles.

Viewing the list of work space members for work space members and non-members can be restricted by the work space administrator.
