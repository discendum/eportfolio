Publicly viewable team work space

If you tick this checkbox, most sections of your team work space will be visible to anyone who visits your work space - including people who do not have an account on this site.

Everyone will be able to see all discussions in the forums. However, people will still have to become a member of the work space before they can post to them. The listing of work space members may be restricted by the work space administrator.

Regardless of this setting, your work space files area and all files inside it will remain visible only to work space members.
